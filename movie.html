1 Gabbar Singh HIT Gabbar Singh is a 2012 Indian Telugu-language action comedy film directed by Harish Shankar. The film stars Pawan Kalyan and Shruti Haasan. Devi Sri Prasad composed the film's music and Jayanan Vincent was the cinematographer.It is a remake of the Hindi film Dabangg (2010) with some major changes in screenplay and characterisations.
2 BRO HIT Bro (transl.Brother) is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language supernatural fantasy comedy film directed by Samuthirakani, from a screenplay by Trivikram Srinivas. Produced by People Media Factory and Zee Studios, it stars Sai Dharam Tej and Pawan Kalyan. It is a remake of director's own 2021 Tamil-language film Vinodhaya Sitham.
3 KATAMARAYUDU HIT Katamarayudu is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language action drama film directed by Kishore Kumar Pardasani. It stars Pawan Kalyan and Shruti Haasan while Tarun Arora, Siva Balaji, Ajay, Ali, Kamal Kamaraju, Chaitanya Krishna, Pradeep Rawat, Rao Ramesh, and Nassar appear in supporting roles.
4 BHEEMLA NAYAK HIT Bheemla Nayak is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Saagar K Chandra from a screenplay by Trivikram Srinivas. It is an official remake of the 2020 Malayalam film Ayyappanum Koshiyum. The film stars Pawan Kalyan and Rana Daggubati in lead roles.
5 JALSA HIT Jalsa (transl.Fun) is a 2008 Indian Telugu-language action comedy film written and directed by Trivikram Srinivas and produced by Allu Aravind, under the Geetha Arts banner. The film stars Pawan Kalyan, Ileana, Parvati Melton and Kamalinee Mukerji, while Mukesh Rishi, Prakash Raj, Brahmanandam, Ali, and Sunil play supporting roles. The soundtrack of the film, composed by Devi Sri Prasad, was launched on 29 February 2008. The film was released on 1 April 2008.
